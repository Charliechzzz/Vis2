Educational level in Western Australia and New South Wales on 2019

Introduction

In recent years, education has been paid more and more attention like the government hopes to improve the level of science and technology

This visualization will start from introducing primary schools, secondary schools and universities in Western Australia and New South Wales.

The datasets will be presented by graphs and be evaluated by certain indies quantatively and qualitatively. The datasets are from Schools and Ranking data - Australia.

Quantative evaluations of educational levels

Primary schools and secondary schools in New South Wales and Western Australia

There are some scoring evaluations to present educational level including Bettereducation score and Index of Community Socio-Educational Advantage (ICSEA) score.

Visulasation idioms like maps, the isotype dot plot, the bar chart and the radial pie chart are presented for these indicies.

Like following map is to show Bettereducation scores on 2019 of every primary secondary schools in Western Australia and New South Wales.

Bettereducation score

Bettereducation score is one of grading algorithms to get the relative educational levels. The map is using Bettereducation score datasets of every primary schools and secondary schools from Bettereducation. The higher score means better eduacational level.

Most of primary and secondary schools area at the coastal cities. By comparision, there are more schools in New South Wales than those in Western Australia. Meanwhile, the number of New South Wales schools gettingg higher Bettereducation score is also larger.

The datasets are calculated to general numerical results. One is the median Bettereducation scores of primary and secondary schools, which are 83 and 82 in New South Wales, and those are up to 85 and 86.5 in Western Australia.

In addition, the ratio of schools whose bettereducation Score > 90 also are considered.This ratio of Western Australia is still the highest, which proves that Western Australia has a great performance on education not only median aspect but also top aspect.

Index of Community Socio-Educational Advantage score

The Index of Community Socio-educational Advantage (ICSEA) is a scale of socio-educational advantage which is the level of educational advantage or disadvantage that students bring to their academic studies. The used dataset is from My School.

It can be seen that the distribution of the top Index of Community Socio-Educational Advantage score is also concentrated at coastal cities. Overall, the map shows New South Wales primary and secondary schools are more competetive.

The Socio-Educational Advantage (SEA) quarter statistics are showing as radial pie charts on the right, percentage of four levels based on Index of Socio-Educational Advantage score.

Universities in New South Wales and Western Australia

The New South Wales and Western Australia universities in Top 500 Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) world university rankings are counted from Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) world university rankings.
